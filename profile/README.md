# ⚒️ Hephaestus' Workshop

## Forge Knowledge. Build Skill. Share Wisdom.

Welcome to Hephaestus' Workshop, a collaborative engineering organization dedicated to learning through design, experimentation, documentation, and practical application.

Inspired by Hephaestus, the craftsman and builder of Greek mythology, this workshop exists for engineers, technicians, students, makers, and lifelong learners seeking to develop their skills through hands-on projects and shared knowledge.

Our philosophy is simple:

> The best way to learn engineering is to build something.

---

# Mission

Hephaestus' Workshop exists to create, document, and share practical engineering knowledge.

Through real-world projects, educational resources, technical discussions, and collaborative development, we strive to make engineering skills more accessible and to foster a culture of continuous learning.

# Current Projects

### Low Voltage Test System

A configurable automated continuity and wiring verification platform for electrical harness validation.

### Solar Tracker and Emulator

An educational renewable energy platform demonstrating photovoltaic systems, solar tracking, and embedded control.

### Portable Motor Controller

A handheld battery-powered tool for motor actuation, troubleshooting, and validation.

### MAESTRO

**Musical Assistance and Educational System for Technique, Repetition, and Optimization**

A wearable haptic-assisted training platform designed to improve finger placement, coordination, timing, and muscle memory for musicians.

# How We Work

Every project follows a general engineering lifecycle:

```text
Idea
 ↓
Research
 ↓
Requirements
 ↓
Design
 ↓
Prototype
 ↓
Testing
 ↓
Documentation
 ↓
Release
```

We believe that documentation is just as important as implementation.

Projects are encouraged to include:

* Requirements
* Schematics
* CAD models
* Source code
* Bills of Materials
* Test procedures
* Validation results
* Lessons learned
